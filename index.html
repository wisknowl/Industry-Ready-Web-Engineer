💡 The Core Training Philosophy

Fundamentals first, automation second. Most programs teach templates and AI shortcuts immediately, producing developers who write code they do not comprehend. In this curriculum, you build applications manually first. When we introduce Laravel, Windsurf, or Cursor, you will understand exactly what they are automating under the hood, making you an independent problem-solver.
